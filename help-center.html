Frequently Asked Questions

Getting started is easy! Simply create an account, complete your profile, and you can start applying for jobs or posting projects right away. We recommend verifying your email and adding a profile picture to increase trust with potential clients or freelancers.

Our payment protection system ensures that funds are securely held in escrow until the project milestones are completed and approved. This protects both clients and freelancers throughout the project lifecycle.

We charge a competitive service fee that varies based on your total earnings with clients. The more you earn with a client, the lower the fee percentage becomes. This encourages long-term relationships and higher earnings.
